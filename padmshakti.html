UI/UX Design • E-commerce Website

PADMSHAKTI

A modern e-commerce platform designed for a premium gifting brand, focusing on personalized gifting experiences, seamless product discovery, and elegant visual storytelling across corporate, event, and personal needs.

Overview
Problem Statement

Most gifting platforms are cluttered, generic, and lack emotional connection. Users struggle to find meaningful, customized gifts while businesses need scalable solutions for bulk and corporate gifting.

Solution

Designed a clean, premium e-commerce experience that simplifies gifting through intuitive navigation, curated categories, and personalization-focused flows. The platform blends emotional storytelling with functional shopping.

Execution
Key Features
  • Smart Product Categorization (Corporate, Event, Personalized)
  • Advanced Filters & Sorting for quick discovery
  • Personalized Gifting Experience
  • Seamless Cart & Checkout Flow
  • User Profile & Order Management System
  • Trust-focused sections (quality, delivery)
Design Style
  • Tone: Premium, modern, trustworthy
  • Colors: Very Peri (primary) with soft neutrals
  • Typography: Clean, modern sans-serif
  • UI Elements: Structured layouts, minimal clutter, elegant spacing
My Role (UX Designer)
  • Designed complete user journey from discovery to checkout
  • Created wireframes and high-fidelity UI screens
  • Defined visual identity aligned with brand tone
  • Designed scalable system for future expansion
Tools Used

Figma

Outcome

A structured and visually refined e-commerce experience that improves product discovery, enhances user trust, and supports both individual and bulk gifting needs in a seamless way.

Important Insight

This project demonstrates end-to-end e-commerce UX thinking — not just UI. It includes complex flows like filtering, checkout, and personalization, which shows strong real-world product design capability beyond basic screens.
