Chapter 01

The Self-Taught Developer

Started my Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) at Atmiya University. While the lecture halls explained theoretical principles, I realized I wanted to build, deploy, and learn faster than the structured curriculum could move.

I dove into Full Stack Development independently. I turned late nights into coding labs, learning frontend layout logics, backend servers, databases, and continuous hosting environments.

"While everyone focused on code, I became obsessed with why the button was there."

2024
Chapter 02

Flutter Changed Everything

Mobile applications were growing rapidly, and I decided to pick up Flutter to learn cross-platform development. I designed and coded several functional apps, testing local databases, state management, and device integration.

But the real shift happened under the hood. As I designed the views for these applications, I found myself constantly questioning button alignments, screen density, click flows, and readability metrics.

I stumbled upon UI/UX Design as a discipline. It was the spark that redirected my career trajectory from pure engineering to designing human-centered products.

Interface Prototypes Flutter & Figma Exploration
