SubStracker

A clean and intuitive mobile application designed to help users track, manage, and optimize their subscription spending. The app focuses on clarity, minimalism, and ease of use.

Overview
Problem Statement

With the rise of subscription-based services, users often lose track of their expenses, forget renewal dates, and continue paying for unused services. Existing solutions are either too complex or fail to present information clearly.

Solution

Designed a minimal and user-friendly subscription management app that provides a clear overview of spending, smart renewal tracking, and effortless subscription management through intuitive UI and structured hierarchy.

Execution
Key Features
  • Smart Dashboard showing monthly & yearly spending
  • Subscription tracking with renewal reminders
  • Add & manage custom subscriptions
  • Multiple sorting options (recent, renewal, duration, price)
  • Category & Payment method management system
  • Clean and persuasive premium (Pro) upgrade flow
Design Style
  • Tone: Clean, modern, minimal, structured
  • Colors: Soft neutrals with subtle accent highlights
  • Typography: Clear, readable, modern sans-serif
  • Experience Focus: Clarity, low cognitive load, fast usability
My Role (UX Designer)
  • Designed complete end-to-end user flow
  • Created wireframes and high-fidelity UI screens
  • Built consistent design system and components
  • Designed complex flows (add, edit, cancel subscriptions)
  • Structured information for better financial readability
  • Designed premium paywall experience
Tools Used

Figma

Outcome

A complete subscription management experience that simplifies financial tracking, improves user awareness, and provides a structured yet minimal interface for managing recurring expenses efficiently.

Important Insight

This project demonstrates functional UX problem-solving combined with system design thinking — not just visual UI. It showcases your ability to design complex data-driven interfaces, structure information clearly, and handle real-world financial use cases.
